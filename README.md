# Pricing_VS_Satisfication Exception Detection Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard identifies products that are underperforming despite being priced above their category average. Using only DAX measures, the dashboard highlights products with high prices, low customer ratings, high risk scores, and weak sales performance. These products are classified as **Critical Business Failures**, enabling managers to take corrective actions before they impact profitability and customer trust.

---

## Objective
The objective of this project is to detect products that require immediate business attention by comparing each product against category-level benchmarks. The dashboard helps decision-makers identify overpriced, low-rated, high-risk products with poor sales performance.

---

## Exception Detection Criteria

A product is marked as a **Critical Business Failure** if it satisfies all of the following conditions:

-  Product Price > Category Average Price
-  Product Rating < Category Average Rating
-  Risk Score belongs to the Top 20% of all products
-  Sales < Category Median Sales

Products meeting all four conditions are flagged as critical exceptions.

---

##  Business Recommendations

For products classified as **Critical Business Failures**, an e-commerce manager should consider:

- **Re-pricing:** Reduce prices to align with customer expectations and market value.
- **Quality Improvement:** Improve product quality or customer experience to increase ratings.
- **Product Removal:** Discontinue products that consistently underperform and negatively impact profitability and brand reputation.
